Welcome to the Radiation Transport and Reactor Physics research group at Oregon State University

Our PI is Dr. Todd Palmer. We are located in the Radiation Center on the Oregon State campus. Our research group is primarily focused on reactor physics, but we are a rather diverse bunch.
